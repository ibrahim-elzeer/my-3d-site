What is The Motherboard

The motherboard is the main circuit board inside a computer. It connects all the components together—like the CPU, RAM, storage, graphics card, and power supply—so they can talk to each other and work as one system. It’s basically the central nervous system of your PC.

Kinds of Motherboard

Motherboard Types

There are several types of motherboards, each designed for different purposes and form factors. Here are some common types:

ATX (Advanced Technology eXtended)

The most common motherboard size, used in standard desktop computers. It offers a good balance of features and expandability.
